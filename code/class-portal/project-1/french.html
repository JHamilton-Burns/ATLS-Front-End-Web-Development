French Open

(aka Roland Garros) was founded in 1891 and as of 2022, has taken place for 126 years. The French Open is the only grand slam played on clay courts which generates a slower paced game with higher ball bounces. The tournament takes place for 2 weeks each year in Paris, France.

Nike

In 2022, Nike sponsored the most players (roughly 70), giving way to tournaments filled with inevitable “swoosh vs. swoosh showdowns. Nike sponsors top tier stars like Naomi Osaka, Rafael Nadal, Nick Kyrgios and formerly, Serena Williams. Pictured is Nike's male and female attire curated for Roland Garros in 2020, 2021, and 2022.

Adidas

Although Adidas sponsors less players than Nike, they still receive quite a bit of recognition from their nearly 60 sponsored players. Big names like Dominic Thiem, Alexander Zverev, and Stefanos Tsisipas earn Adidas a spot at the top in tennis fashion.
